Game Instructions

Welcome to Lifer_Lighdow Sci-Fi Clicker Game!

Game Objective:

  • Click the "Click to Earn" button to gain money.
  • Use money to buy and upgrade various buildings (Auto Clickers, Farms, Mines, Banks, Stock Exchanges) to increase passive income.
  • Upon reaching certain conditions, you can "Prestige" to increase your "My Level", which provides higher click and passive income bonuses.
  • "Reset Assets" will reset all buildings and money, but keep "My Level" and increase the level requirement for the next reset.
  • "Full Game Reset" will revert all data to its initial state.

Building Functions:

  • Clicker: Increases the amount of money earned per click.
  • Auto Clickers: Automatically generate money per second.
  • Farms: Generate passive money.
  • Mines: Generate more passive money.
  • Banks: Requires 10 Farms to purchase, provides significant passive money.
  • Stock Exchanges: Requires 5 Mines to purchase, provides massive passive money.

Strategy:

Balance your click income and passive income, allocating resources wisely for purchases and upgrades. When progress slows down, consider performing a Prestige to gain powerful bonuses and accelerate your game!

Note on Prestige vs. Reset Assets:

  • Prestige: Resets your money and all building *quantities* and *levels* back to initial, but *keeps* the current prices of all upgrades and buildings. Your "My Level" increases, providing a permanent multiplier.
  • Reset Assets: Resets your money, all building *quantities*, *levels*, AND *prices* back to their initial values. Your "My Level" is kept, but the requirement for the next "Reset Assets" increases. This is useful for starting a fresh economic cycle with accumulated "My Level" benefits.
